Christiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo continues to set and break records as he continues to cement himself as one of the greatest to ever play the game. That continued Saturday as the Portuguese superstar scored a hat-trick against Tottenham, adding his name to another top list and setting himself on a potential collision course yet another record. Ronaldo became the second-oldest player to ever score a Premier League hat-trick with his trio of goals against Tottenham over the weekend. He is just over 100 days behind the record, and could set the mark if he stays with Manchester United this summer and continues in the Premier League next season.
